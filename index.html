SUDOKU SOLVER

Note: The three primary conditions that must be satisfied for a valid Sudoku puzzle are:
1. Row Condition: Each row in the 9x9 grid must contain the digits 1 to 9 without repetition.
2. Column Condition: Each column in the 9x9 grid must contain the digits 1 to 9 without repetition.
3. Subgrid (3x3 Box) Condition: The grid is divided into nine 3x3 subgrids. Each of these subgrids must also contain the digits 1 to 9 without repetition.
